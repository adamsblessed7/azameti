        <h2>Research Profile</h2>
        <p> Adams A. K. Azameti was born in Accra, Ghana. He received his MPhil in computer science from the University of Ghana, in 2018, specializing in Wireless Communication & Intelligent Systems. His research interest spans Artificial Intelligence, Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS), Software Engineering, Protocol Design, and Robotics. He works on Artificial Intelligence (AI), and Machine Learning (ML), with an AI/ML Policy focus Education.  His MPhil thesis was in Improving Intelligent Transportation Systems, (ITS), through Analytical Investigation of Macroscopic Traffic Flow Model in Vehicular Ad Hoc Networks, VANETS. He has published four (4) papers on Intelligent Wireless Communication Systems and Software Engineering. He is currently a lecturer at the University of Professional Studies. He has several years of teaching experience in Ghana's tertiary institutions and senior high schools. He is a Peer-Reviewer for the Journal of IEEE Transactions on Vehicular Technology, Elsevier Vehicular Communications, Elsevier Array Journal of Machine-to-Machine Communications, journals of EAI Endorsed Transactions on Mobile Communications and Applications, and EAI Endorsed Transactions on Pervasive Health and Technology and more. He is a professional member of the IEE and ACM.</p>
